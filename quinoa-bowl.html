Quinoa Bowl

Our Quinoa Bowl is a nutritious blend of quinoa, roasted vegetables, and fresh greens, topped with a drizzle of tahini dressing and a sprinkle of seeds.

Ingredients:

  • Quinoa
  • Roasted vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, carrots)
  • Mixed greens
  • Tahini dressing
  • Sunflower seeds
