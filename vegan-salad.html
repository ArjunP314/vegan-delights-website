Vegan Salad

Our Vegan Salad is a vibrant mix of fresh, organic greens, cucumbers, tomatoes, carrots, and bell peppers, all tossed in a zesty lemon dressing and sprinkled with crunchy nuts.

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens (spinach, arugula, lettuce)
  • Cucumbers
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Bell peppers
  • Lemon dressing
  • Mixed nuts
