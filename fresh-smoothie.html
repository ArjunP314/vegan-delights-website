Fresh Smoothie

Our Fresh Smoothie is a revitalizing blend of seasonal fruits and leafy greens, served chilled for a refreshing burst of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Seasonal fruits (e.g., banana, berries, mango)
  • Leafy greens (e.g., spinach, kale)
  • Plant-based milk (e.g., almond milk)
  • Chia seeds
  • Ice cubes
